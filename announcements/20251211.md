11 December update on Raku Steering Council status
--------------------------------------------------

John Haltiwanger (ab5tract) has accepted the invitation by the Raku Steering Council to become a member.

The remaining council members have taken a vote whether to accept John Haltiwanger into the Raku Steering Council.  This vote was unanimously accepted.

Therefore the current Raku Steering Council now consists of (in alphabetical order):

- Geoffrey Broadwell
- Richard Hainsworth
- John Haltiwanger
- Nick Logan
- Elizabeth Mattijsen
- Stefan Seifert
- Daniel Sockwell
