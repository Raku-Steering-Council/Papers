Notes:

Voting technically should have ended as Sep-20 started, but due to the potentially
confusing use of "midnight", voting was left open until the end of the 20th. A
small number of ballots were received during these last 24 hours.

Several ballots were submitted by people without commit bits. Discussion on #raku on
IRC resulted in inviting some of the voters an invitation to have a commit bit based
on their history of activity in the community.

Users who were sent an invitation (which occurred before the end of voting period)
had their ballots counted.
